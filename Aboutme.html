Who am I?

I see myself as a curious person.

I’ve always been obsessed with understanding how things work. How is this tech created? How does it work? That naturally led me to think creatively and push boundaries. I pride myself on being an out-of-the-box thinker. I search, I research. I solve and resolve. I try – sometimes fail – but I always retry. I believe there is always something new to learn and improve on, and that mindset fuels my journey.
Consistent improvement is something I deeply value and practice.

My Technical Journey

My passion for problem-solving led me into the world of AI and Machine Learning. Over the years, I’ve gained hands-on experience with technologies such as Python, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, and PyTorch, enabling me to build intelligent systems. I’ve become particularly interested in large language models, working with tools like Langchain and Llamaindex, fine-tuning models to solve real-world challenges.

I’ve also developed expertise in web development, using FastAPI and Flutter to create full-stack applications. Whether it's implementing backends with MySQL and MongoDB or designing front-end solutions, I aim to make technology accessible and impactful.
